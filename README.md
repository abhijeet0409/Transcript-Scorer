## âš™ï¸ How It Works

### 1. **Provide the Transcript**

You can choose one of the following:

* **Upload a file** containing your transcript.
* **Paste or write** your transcript in the input area.

### 2. **AI Processing**

Once the inputs are provided:

### 3. **Scoring & Output**

The system generates:

* **Parameter-wise score breakdown**
* **Strengths and weaknesses**
* **Accuracy and coherence analysis**
* **Improvement suggestions**
* **Overall score** out of 100
